Why I Created Driveway Auctions

As a lifelong car enthusiast, I've always believed that every car has a story worth telling and a future worth exploring. Too often, I watched incredible project cars and high-mileage heroes get overlooked by traditional auction houses that only focus on pristine classics or low-kilometer specimens.

I created Driveway Auctions because I saw a gap in the market – a place where the forgotten gems, the weekend warriors, and the diamond-in-the-rough vehicles could find their perfect match. These aren't just cars with problems; they're opportunities waiting for the right person who can see their potential.

Whether it's a barn find that needs some love, a high-mileage daily driver with years of reliable service ahead, or a project car that's 90% complete and just needs the right enthusiast to cross the finish line, every vehicle deserves a chance to continue its journey.

Driveway Auctions is more than just a marketplace – it's a community of passionate people who understand that the best automotive stories often begin with "I know it doesn't look like much, but..."
